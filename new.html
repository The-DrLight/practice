Choosing a Career in Tech

Why choose tech?

What excites you about tech? What are your strength?. Tech is too wide for you to jump in.
Networking and Cybersecurity are good use of tech. Frontend, backend, full stack, mobile, security, networking, operating system, server management are all branches of tech. Frontend and backend are embled in full stack.

  • Set clear and realistic goals.
  • Research and learn.
  • Understand the tech landscale.
  • Research roles and functions
  • Utilize free online resources.
  • Read books.
  • Develop core skills
  • Learn a language
  • Learn the fundamentals.
  • Collect deals
  • Don't be afraid to work for free.
  • Build practical experience
  • Volunteer for meaningful projectcs
  • Get certification
  • Join communities.
  • Resume and Job application; apply for intern and entry level positions(entry level takes three to four years experience).
